# Tools

Figma is home base, but a few other tools do specific jobs.

## PostHog, product analytics

We use **PostHog** for product analytics and data. When you need to understand or track
how people move through a certain flow, that's where you look.

<Callout type="note">
  The **Analytics** Figma file is a *log* of older PostHog analytics. It's legacy and no
  longer in active use. For live data, go to PostHog directly.
</Callout>

## FigJam, whiteboard and diagrams

For whiteboarding and diagrams (flows, maps, loose thinking) reach for **FigJam**.

## Gemini, image generation

**Gemini** generates the source images for achievements. From there they get brought
into the component properly. See the [Achievements recipe](/handbook/recipes/achievements).
