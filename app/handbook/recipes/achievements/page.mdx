# Recipe – Achievements

How a new achievement goes from an idea to a usable in-app asset.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate the image">
    Start in **Gemini** and generate the achievement artwork as an image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add it to the component">
    Bring the image into the **Achievements component** as a proper **variant**, so it
    can be used in the app like every other achievement. This is the important part: it
    has to live in the component, not float as a loose image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prep the image correctly">
    Three things matter, every time (see below).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The image must be right

<Callout type="warning" title="Non-negotiables for every achievement image">
  - **1:1 aspect ratio.** Square, always.
  - **Small file size.** Keep it light. Don't ship a huge image.
  - **A dark variant.** Every achievement needs one.
</Callout>

### Making the dark variant

The dark variant is produced from the same image by pushing three adjustments to their
extremes:

- **Contrast to max**
- **Saturation to zero**
- **Exposure to zero**

That combination gives the consistent dark-mode treatment used across all achievements.
